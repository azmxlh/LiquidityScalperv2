1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

The Liquidity Scalper EA is a fully automated trading system designed 
specifically for XAUUSD (Gold) based on institutional order flow principles.

WHAT THIS EA DOES:
✓ Identifies liquidity sweeps above/below key levels
✓ Waits for displacement (strong directional move)
✓ Enters on retest of order blocks
✓ Places continuation stop orders on trend breaks
✓ Manages trades automatically (breakeven, partials, trailing)
✓ Enforces strict risk limits (daily loss, max trades, news filter)

WHAT THIS EA DOES NOT DO:
✗ Grid trading or martingale
✗ Revenge trading after losses
✗ Trade during high-impact news
✗ Hold positions overnight
✗ Overtrade (respects session/daily limits)

2. STRATEGY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

CORE CONCEPT: Market Microstructure

The EA exploits the predictable sequence of:
LIQUIDITY SWEEP → DISPLACEMENT → RETEST

WHY THIS WORKS ON GOLD:

1. LIQUIDITY POOLS
   - Gold has massive liquidity at obvious levels
   - Institutions need liquidity to fill large orders
   - They "hunt" stop losses above/below key levels

2. DISPLACEMENT
   - After sweeping liquidity, price moves aggressively
   - This signals institutional positioning
   - Creates "fair value gaps" and order blocks

3. RETEST
   - Price often returns to test the breakout zone
   - This offers low-risk, high-reward entries
   - Order blocks act as support/resistance

THE EDGE:
We're not predicting - we're REACTING to institutional footprints.

3. HOW THE EA WORKS (Technical Flow)
================================================================================

DAILY PREPARATION (Before 08:00 UTC):

1. Calculate key levels:
   - Previous Day High/Low (PDH/PDL)
   - Asian Session High/Low (ASH/ASL)
   - Day opening price (midnight UTC)
   - Round number levels

2. Analyze H1 structure:
   - Is market bullish (HH/HL)?
   - Is market bearish (LH/LL)?
   - Is market ranging?

3. Draw levels on chart
4. Reset daily counters (trades, P/L)

---

REAL-TIME MONITORING (Every M5 candle):

STEP 1: Pre-flight checks
- Am I in trading session? (London 08-12, NY 13:30-17)
- Is daily loss limit reached? (-2%)
- Max trades hit? (5/day, 3/session)
- Is news coming? (within 15 min)

If ANY check fails → Skip trading, manage existing trades only

---

STEP 2: Model A - Liquidity Sweep Scanner

A. DETECT SWEEP
   - Did price sweep ASH/PDH (for shorts)?
   - Did price sweep ASL/PDL (for longs)?
   - Did price REVERSE back inside range?
   
   If YES → Liquidity sweep confirmed

B. DETECT DISPLACEMENT
   - Is next candle >80 points range?
   - Did it break previous structure?
   - Is direction aligned with sweep reversal?
   
   If YES → Displacement confirmed

C. IDENTIFY ORDER BLOCK
   - Find last opposite candle before displacement
   - For longs: last bearish candle (bullish OB)
   - For shorts: last bullish candle (bearish OB)
   
   Mark this zone for entry

D. WAIT FOR RETEST
   - Is price back in order block zone?
   - Has it been <10 candles since displacement?
   
   If YES → EXECUTE TRADE

---

STEP 3: Model B - Stop Order Scanner

A. DETECT BREAK OF STRUCTURE (BOS)
   - Check H1 for clear trend direction
   - Did recent M5 candle break previous high/low?
   
   If YES → BOS confirmed

B. DETECT CONSOLIDATION
   - Are last 5 candles in <200 point range?
   - Has price paused after BOS?
   
   If YES → Consolidation confirmed

C. PLACE STOP ORDER
   - Bullish: BUY STOP 5 points above consolidation high
   - Bearish: SELL STOP 5 points below consolidation low
   - Set SL at opposite side of consolidation
   - Set 2-hour expiry
   
   Order placed, wait for trigger

---

STEP 4: Trade Management (Active trades)

For each open position:

A. BREAKEVEN
   - If profit >100 points → Move SL to entry +2
   
B. PARTIAL CLOSE (TP1)
   - If profit ≥1.5x risk → Close 50% of position
   
C. TRAILING STOP
   - If profit >100 points → Trail SL using M5 swings
   - Keep 25-point buffer
   
D. SESSION CLOSE
   - If time ≥17:00 UTC → Close all positions
   
E. EARLY EXIT
   - If M5 structure breaks against me → Close immediately

---

STEP 5: End of Day

- Close all positions at 17:00 UTC
- Delete pending orders
- Update statistics
- Prepare for next day

5. RISK MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

POSITION SIZING FORMULA:

Lot Size = (Account Balance × Risk%) / (Stop Loss in $ per lot)

EXAMPLE:
- Account: $10,000
- Risk per trade: 1%
- Stop loss: 100 points ($10/lot for XAUUSD)

Lot Size = ($10,000 × 0.01) / ($10) = 0.10 lots

---

DAILY LIMITS (NON-NEGOTIABLE):

1. DAILY LOSS LIMIT: -2%
   - If hit → EA stops trading for the day
   - Protects from catastrophic drawdown
   - Resets at midnight UTC

2. DAILY PROFIT TARGET: +3%
   - Optional lock (can disable)
   - If hit → EA stops trading
   - Secures gains, prevents giving back

3. MAX TRADES PER DAY: 5
   - Prevents overtrading
   - Quality over quantity
   - Forces selectivity

4. MAX TRADES PER SESSION: 3
   - Limits exposure per volatile period
   - London: max 3 trades
   - NY: max 3 trades

---

STOP LOSS RULES:

Model A:
- Maximum: 150 points
- Typical: 80-120 points
- Placed below swept low (longs) or above swept high (shorts)

Model B:
- Maximum: 120 points
- Typical: 60-100 points
- Placed outside consolidation range

NEVER:
✗ Move stop loss against position
✗ Remove stop loss
✗ Enter if stop would exceed maximum

---

TAKE PROFIT STRATEGY:

TP1 (50% of position):
- Risk:Reward = 1:1.5 (default)
- Locks in profit
- Moves SL to breakeven

TP2 (Remaining 50%):
- Risk:Reward = 1:3 (default)
- Or trail with M5 structure
- Captures extended moves

6. TRADE MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

BREAKEVEN PROTOCOL:

When to move:
- Trade in profit by 100 points (default)
- OR TP1 has been hit

Where to move:
- Entry price + 2 points (small buffer)

Why:
- Secures trade against reversal
- Allows runner to develop risk-free
- Psychological comfort

---

PARTIAL CLOSE (TP1):

When:
- Profit reaches 1.5x risk (default)
- Example: 100pt SL → TP1 at 150pts profit

How much:
- 50% of position (default, adjustable)

Action after TP1:
- Move SL to breakeven +2
- Let remainder run to TP2 or trail

---

TRAILING STOP:

Activation:
- After profit >100 points
- After TP1 hit

Method:
- Follow M5 swing lows (for longs)
- Follow M5 swing highs (for shorts)
- Maintain 25-point buffer

Update frequency:
- Every new M5 swing formation

Example (Long trade):
- Entry: 2,650
- Current: 2,750 (+100 pts)
- Last M5 swing low: 2,730
- Trail SL to: 2,730 - 25 = 2,705

---

EARLY EXIT CONDITIONS:

Exit IMMEDIATELY if:
☑ M5 closes against structure (candle fully below/above key level)
☑ Unexpected high-impact news announced
☑ Session end (17:00 UTC)
☑ Daily profit target reached
☑ Extreme volatility spike (>200pt wicks)

Exit REMAINDER (after TP1) if:
☑ Price consolidates >6 candles without progression
☑ Opposite setup forms (bearish OB in long trade)
☑ Trail stop within 15 points of price

8. NEWS FILTER
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
Avoid trading during unpredictable high-impact news events.

HIGH-IMPACT EVENTS (Avoid):
- NFP (Non-Farm Payrolls) - First Friday each month
- FOMC (Federal Reserve) - 8 times/year
- CPI (Inflation data) - Monthly
- GDP releases
- Central bank rate decisions
- Unemployment reports

---

HOW IT WORKS:

Manual List:
- Default times in EA settings: "08:30,13:30,15:00"
- These are common US news times (UTC)
- Edit this list based on economic calendar

Time Windows:
- Minutes before news: 15 (default)
- Minutes after news: 15 (default)
- Example: If news at 13:30, EA stops at 13:15, resumes at 13:45

---

WEEKLY ROUTINE:

Every Sunday:
1. Check ForexFactory economic calendar
2. Identify all "red folder" (high impact) events for the week
3. Note exact UTC times
4. Update EA parameter if needed

Example week:
Monday: No major news
Tuesday: 13:30 UTC - CPI (add to list)
Wednesday: 19:00 UTC - FOMC (add to list)
Thursday: No major news
Friday: 13:30 UTC - NFP (already in default list)

Updated parameter:
"08:30,13:30,19:00"

---

OVERRIDE:

If you want to trade through news (NOT RECOMMENDED):
- Set EnableNewsFilter = false
- Risk increases significantly
- Violates prop firm best practices

13. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)
================================================================================

Q: Why does the EA sometimes not take trades for hours?
A: This is normal. The EA waits for HIGH-QUALITY setups only. Some days 
   only 1-2 setups form. Quality > quantity. Do not force trades.

Q: I got 5 losses in a row. Is the EA broken?
A: No. With a 65% win rate, you'll have losing streaks. 5 losses can happen
   by pure statistics. Stick to the plan, don't revenge trade.

Q: Can I run this on multiple charts/pairs?
A: NO. This EA is ONLY for XAUUSD. Do not run on other pairs. You can run
   multiple instances on different XAUUSD accounts with different magic numbers.

Q: The EA placed a trade right before news!
A: Check your news filter settings. Verify times are in UTC and correctly
   entered. Update the list weekly based on economic calendar.

Q: Why does TP1 sometimes not get hit?
A: Market volatility. Sometimes price moves too fast, or reverses before
   reaching TP1. This is why we use stop loss - not every trade is a winner.

Q: Can I manually close trades?
A: Yes, but NOT RECOMMENDED. Let the EA manage trades. Manual intervention
   often leads to emotional decisions and worse results.

Q: The dashboard shows wrong levels?
A: Verify your broker's server time is UTC-based. Some brokers use GMT+2/3.
   If persistent, contact broker to confirm server time.

Q: I want to increase risk to 5% to grow faster?
A: DO NOT. Even professionals rarely exceed 2% per trade. Higher risk =
   higher chance of blowing account. Slow and steady wins.

Q: Can I change the timeframe to M1 or H1?
A: NO. The EA is coded for M5 execution. Changing timeframe will break the
   logic. Keep on M5.

Q: How do I backtest further than 3 months?
A: Download historical data from your broker or use sites like Dukascopy.
   Import into MT4. However, recent data (3-6 months) most relevant.

Q: The EA keeps hitting daily loss limit?
A: This suggests either:
   1. Parameters not optimized for current market
   2. Broker spread too wide
   3. Slippage issues
   4. Need to reduce risk or tighten filters
   Review settings and consider demo testing.

Q: Can I use this with other EAs?
A: Yes, but ensure different magic numbers. However, combining strategies
   can complicate risk management. Better to master one system first.

Detachment:
- Don't watch every tick
- Set EA and check once per session
- Overmonitoring leads to interference

Journaling:
- Note market conditions (trending, ranging, choppy)
- Record your emotional state before trading day
- Identify patterns (e.g., "I trade poorly on Mondays")

Routine:
- Same pre-market routine daily
- Reduces decision fatigue
- Builds discipline

Celebrate wins, analyze losses:
- Take profit when TP hit, enjoy it
- When loss occurs, review objectively
- Ask "Did EA follow rules?" not "Why did I lose?"

ADVANCED RISK MANAGEMENT:
--------------------------

Correlation risk:
- Never hold >2 positions simultaneously
- If 2 longs open, very high correlation risk
- Consider partial close on one if both in profit

Time-based risk:
- Reduce risk on Fridays (weekend risk)
- First trading day of month: 0.5% risk only
- Major news days: skip trading entirely

Drawdown ladder:
- 0-3% DD: 1% risk
- 3-5% DD: 0.75% risk
- 5-8% DD: 0.5% risk
- >8% DD: stop trading, review

Dynamic position sizing:
- After 5 consecutive wins: reduce risk slightly (avoid overconfidence)
- After 3 consecutive losses: reduce risk (rebuild confidence)
- Return to normal after 2 trades
