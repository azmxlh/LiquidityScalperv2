STEP 1: INSTALL (5 minutes)
================================================================================

1. Download all EA files to your computer
2. Open MT4, press F4 (opens MetaEditor)
3. File → Open Data Folder
4. Put LiquidityScalper_MT4.mq4 in: MQL4/Experts folder
5. Put LS_Functions.mqh and LS_Config.mqh in: MQL4/Include folder
6. Close and reopen MT4
7. Open MetaEditor (F4), find LiquidityScalper_MT4, click Compile
8. Should see "0 errors" at bottom

✓ Done? Files installed!

================================================================================
STEP 2: ENABLE TRADING (2 minutes)
================================================================================

1. Tools → Options → Expert Advisors tab
2. Check ALL boxes (Allow automated trading, Allow DLL imports, etc.)
3. Click OK
4. Look at top toolbar - click "AutoTrading" button (should turn GREEN)

✓ Done? EA can now trade!

STEP 8: COMMON FIRST-DAY QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: EA hasn't opened any trades after 3 hours?
A: NORMAL. EA waits for high-quality setups. Some days only 1-2 trades.

Q: I want to manually close a trade?
A: DON'T. Let EA manage it. Manual intervention often makes things worse.

Q: Can I change settings mid-day?
A: You CAN, but shouldn't. Change settings at end of day only.

Q: EA opened a trade right before news!
A: Check your news times are correct and in UTC. Update the parameter.

Q: I got a losing trade, should I increase risk to recover?
A: NO! Stick to 0.5-1% risk. Revenge trading destroys accounts.

Q: How much should I make per day?
A: Don't think in daily targets. Think monthly: 15-30% per month realistic.
